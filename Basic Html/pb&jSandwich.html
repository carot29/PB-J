PB&J Recipe

Learn how to make a simple Peanut Butter and jelly sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • peanut butter
  • jelly (grape jelly)

Instructions

  1. Lay out 2 slices of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on one slice.
  3. Spread jelly on the other slice
  4. Put the two slices together
